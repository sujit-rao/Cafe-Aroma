About Us

Who We Are

Welcome to Café Aroma, where every cup tells a story. Nestled in the heart of Evergreen Heights, our cozy coffee shop is more than just a place to grab a cup of coffee—it's a community hub, a haven for coffee lovers, and a retreat from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. At Café Aroma, we believe that coffee is an art form. Our journey began with a simple love for the perfect brew, and it has grown into a passion for sharing that love with you. From the moment you step through our doors, you'll be greeted by the rich aroma of freshly ground beans and the warm smiles of our baristas, who are dedicated to crafting the perfect cup every time.

At Café Aroma, we're proud to be a part of the Evergreen Heights community. We host regular events, from live music to coffee tasting workshops, and we're always looking for ways to give back. We believe that a coffee shop should be a place where everyone feels welcome, and we strive to create a space where you can feel at home. Experience the warmth of our hospitality and the richness of our brews.
